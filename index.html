A little bit about myself

I would describe myself as very open-minded. I love doing a lot of activities. I've played soccer my entire life - from youth league to college soccer to adult leagues. I also love hiking with my German Shepherd, fishing, visiting breweries, camping, and many other outdoor activities. Despite all of my atheltic activity interests, I also love the technology world. Math has always been such a great passion of mine and so I've decided to persue that degree. I've also been obsessed with designing, computers, and web development. Most of my knowledge comes from a school and work basis, but I always spend hours at home messing with my page to tweak it a little and eventually learn new skills.

I work as a Computer Consultant and a Deisgn and Production Studio member for the University of New Mexico for the department of College of Education. My specific department is with the Center for Student success. I assist on a wide variety of technical issues to students, faculty, and staff for the Center for Student Success (CSS). I also provide basic troubleshooting of computer hardware, software, printers and projectors and support special projects in the CSS utilizing technical expertise and knowledge of the College of Educations (COE). I demonstrate excellent customer service skills and interest in teaching, communicating, and furthering understanding of computer use specific to the systems utilized by the COE and CSS. I work with our Network Systems Analyst and Support Analyst to resolve online tickets for technical issues and I administrate multiple COE accounts for troubleshooting, configuring, installations, etc. Lastly, I also work on a team to design images, flyers, posters, ID cards for advertisement and helped code for our CSS Portal Website. This job has been nothing but a blessing to me.
